Overview

The challenge

A new business launching in Blantyre needed video content that would make a strong first impression on social media. They had raw footage of their products and space, but no animated branding or motion identity to tie it all together.

The goal was to create a polished motion package that elevated their raw footage into a broadcast-quality promotional campaign for the launch.

Process

How I approached it

01

Brief & Storyboard

Worked closely with the client to understand their brand tone — energetic but premium. Created a storyboard mapping each scene, transition, and text moment in the 30-second hero video before any animation began.

02

Motion Identity Design

Designed static assets in Illustrator — logo variants, text lockups, and graphic elements — then brought them to life in After Effects with eased animations, kinetic typography, and smooth transitions.

03

Video Assembly

Assembled the client's raw footage with the motion graphics in Premiere Pro. Added colour grading, sound design, and timing adjustments to create a polished, broadcast-ready final cut.

04

Asset Delivery

Exported all 5 video assets in multiple formats optimised for Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and WhatsApp Status. Provided a reusable After Effects template so future video content stays on-brand.

Outcome

The result

The launch campaign exceeded the client's expectations — the hero video reached thousands of people organically in the first week, and the business opened to a packed crowd who had seen the content online.
